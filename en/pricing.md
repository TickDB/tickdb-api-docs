## Choosing a Plan

- **Free** — Quick evaluation, no credit card required
- **Basic** — Individual developers and small projects
- **Pro** — Quant teams and mid-size trading systems, most popular
- **Enterprise** — Institutional use, high concurrency and large-scale data
