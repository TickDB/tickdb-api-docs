## Symbol Naming Rules

- Forex: `EURUSD`
- Metals: `XAUUSD`
- Indices: `SPX`
- US Stocks: `AAPL.US`
- HK Stocks: `700.HK`
- A-Shares: `000001.SZ` (Shenzhen), `600000.SH` (Shanghai)
- Crypto: `BTCUSDT`

## Timestamp

- All timestamps are in **milliseconds (ms)**
- UTC-based
