## Symbol Naming Rules

- Crypto: `BTCUSDT`
- US Stocks: `AAPL.US`
- HK Stocks: `700.HK`
- Forex: `EURUSD`
- Metals: `XAUUSD`

## Timestamp

- All timestamps are in **milliseconds (ms)**
- UTC-based
