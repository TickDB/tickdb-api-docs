---
title: Available Symbols
description: Query all available trading symbols supported by TickDB.
openapi: GET /v1/symbols/available
---

## Supported Markets

- **FOREX** - Foreign Exchange
- **METALS** - Precious Metals
- **INDICES** - Market Indices
- **US** - US Stock Market
- **HK** - Hong Kong Stock Exchange
- **CN** - A-Share Market
- **CRYPTO** - Cryptocurrency Pairs

## Notes
- Market codes are case-insensitive
- Refer to Data Specification for symbol naming conventions
- US, HK, CN stock markets support all listed stock symbols
